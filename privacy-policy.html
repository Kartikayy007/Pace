Pace Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2024

Overview

Pace is a fitness tracking app that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on any external servers.

Data We Access

Pace accesses the following data locally on your device:

  • Location: To track your route during workouts
  • HealthKit: To record workouts, heart rate, and step count
  • Motion: To calculate cadence (steps per minute)

Data Storage

All your data stays on your device and in Apple HealthKit. We do not have servers and do not transmit your data anywhere.

Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third parties. Period.

Your Control

You can revoke Pace's access to HealthKit and Location at any time in your device Settings.
